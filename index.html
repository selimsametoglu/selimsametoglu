I am a last year PhD candidate with a leveraging data from social media and use this in genetically informative designs. I make use of machine learning models (e.g., natural language processing, image to text, and cascaded/combined models) to assess wellbeing at individual and regional levels. As of 2020, I have been working at The Netherlands Twin Register.
